My Digital Backpack privacy policy

My Digital Backpack allows it's users to create an experience profile that they can share online with other people.

The profiles includes personal data:

  • that the user has supplied explicitly including for example name, title, location, birthdate as well as work/education experience
  • that the user has explicitly allowed My Digital Backpack to access from other platforms, for example reputation data from a gig platform.
  • that the user has explicitly allowed My Digital Backpack to access by connecting to a third party data provider, for example we are currently using the service My CV Data.

The profiles can be shared online by sharing a link, this means that anyone with access to the link will be able to see the data. The user should consider any data they upload on their profile as publically available.

My Digital Backpack needs to store all profile data to be able to share profiles. The data will be removed shortly after a user removes their profile.

In addition to the data the user has explicitly supplied, My Digital Backpack also collects tracking data, used to improve the service. This is done using a third party service, currently Google Analytics.

Users can contact info@mycognitionmatters.org if they want to access or delete their data.

My Digital Backpack will occasionally update this privacy policy information, and will then inform all existing users through email.
